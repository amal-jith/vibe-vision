The Story Behind the Beat

We Create Unforgettable Moments!

Founded in 2009 by Aaron Waldock, Vibe & Vision Productions began with a passion for music and entertainment. Starting as a karaoke host, Aaron honed his DJ skills at Dub Academy in Austin, Texas, later gaining experience in the city’s vibrant 6th Street club scene. His love for creating unforgettable moments was sparked when he DJed a friend’s wedding, inspiring him to build a company that blends high-energy music with personalized event experiences.
